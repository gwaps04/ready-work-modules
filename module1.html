VLOOKUP (The Classic)

Fast, compatible with all sheets. Requires the 'Unique Key' to be in the leftmost column.

=VLOOKUP(Key, Range, ColIndex, FALSE)

XLOOKUP (The Modern King)

No column index needed. Can look left, right, up, or down. Handles errors automatically.

=XLOOKUP(Key, SearchCol, ResultCol)
